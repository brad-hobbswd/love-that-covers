Preface

An invitation into a story of healing, grace, and the power of love that restores.

This book began as a reflection on the power of love to heal, restore, and transform. In life, moments of pain, loss, and brokenness often shape the way we see ourselves and others. Yet through it all, God’s love continues to cover what is wounded and gently invites restoration.

Love That Covers reveals the ripple effect of love. One act of compassion, one choice to forgive, and one moment of mercy can extend far beyond what is visible. Through the lives of Miriam, Ruth, Grace, Samuel, and Daniel, the story unfolds as a testimony of how love moves across generations.

This is more than a story. This is an invitation to reflect on the ways love has reached you and the ways you can allow that same love to flow through your life. Healing begins where love is received, and transformation begins where love is given.

May these pages remind you that love is stronger than shame, forgiveness is stronger than resentment, and mercy always has the final word. May your life become a place where love covers, restores, and multiplies.
