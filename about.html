About the Author

A journey of healing, truth, and a love that never lets go.

Bradley Hobbs is a writer, teacher, and storyteller with a deep passion for healing, restoration, and the transforming love of Jesus. His work is shaped by personal experiences of navigating pain, forgiveness, and renewal, offering readers a message grounded in mercy, truth, and hope.

With a heart for those who have felt unseen, silenced, or overlooked, Brad writes to restore a sense of belonging and identity. His voice carries compassion and honesty, inviting readers into a journey where love reaches into broken places and gently brings healing.

Brad holds a background in Information Technology and Business Management and has spent over six years teaching and mentoring both children and adults. His passion for education was deeply influenced by his son, Gabriel, whose life inspired him to step into teaching and invest in others with patience, care, and purpose.

A passionate communicator, Brad believes stories carry a unique power to reach the heart. Through narrative, he reveals that God’s love is not distant or reserved, but active, generational, and transformative. His writing reflects a life shaped through both joy and hardship, where even the most difficult seasons became places of growth and renewal.

In addition to Love That Covers, Brad continues to write devotionals and works for readers of all ages, including children. His mission remains clear: to help others encounter the love of Jesus, embrace their identity, and walk confidently in truth without fear or shame.

When he is not writing, Brad enjoys mentoring, teaching, and creating meaningful spaces where others can experience encouragement and hope. His life and work stand as a reminder that love does more than cover. Love restores, heals, and multiplies across generations.
