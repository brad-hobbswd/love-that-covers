About the Author

Bradley Hobbs has a heart for storytelling, healing, and inspiring others through the power of God’s love. With years of personal experience navigating pain, forgiveness, and restoration, Brad writes to encourage readers to trust in God’s mercy and to embrace love that heals.

A passionate teacher and communicator, Brad believes that stories have the power to reach hearts in ways that words alone cannot. Through narrative, he seeks to show that God’s love is active, generational, and transformative.

When Brad is not writing, he enjoys mentoring, teaching, and finding creative ways to share hope with others. His life and work are a testament to the truth that love covers, restores, and multiplies.
