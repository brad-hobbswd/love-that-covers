About the Author

A voice for healing, identity, and a love that restores what was once broken.

Bradley Hobbs is a writer, teacher, and storyteller with a deep passion for healing, restoration, and the transforming love of Jesus. His work is shaped by personal experiences of navigating pain, forgiveness, and renewal, offering readers a message grounded in mercy, truth, and hope.

With a heart for those who have felt unseen, silenced, or overlooked, Brad writes to restore a sense of belonging and identity. His voice carries compassion and honesty, inviting readers into a journey where love reaches into broken places and gently brings healing.

Brad holds a background in Information Technology and Business Management and has spent over six years teaching and mentoring both children and adults. His passion for education was deeply influenced by his son, Gabriel, whose life inspired him to step into teaching and invest in others with patience, care, and purpose.

A passionate communicator, Brad believes stories carry a unique power to reach the heart. Through narrative, he reveals that God’s love is active, generational, and transformative. His writing reflects a life shaped through both joy and hardship, where even the most difficult seasons became places of growth and renewal.

In addition to Love That Covers, Brad continues to write devotionals and works for readers of all ages. His mission is to help others encounter the love of Jesus, embrace their identity, and walk confidently in truth.

When he is not writing, Brad enjoys mentoring, teaching, and creating meaningful spaces where others can experience encouragement and hope. His life stands as a reminder that love restores, heals, and multiplies.
