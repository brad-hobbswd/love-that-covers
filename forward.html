Forward

Love is more than a feeling. Love is action, mercy, and the quiet power that transforms lives.

There are moments in life when we are reminded that love is more than a feeling. It is an action, a choice, and sometimes a quiet sacrifice that changes everything. Love That Covers is a story of love that sees pain, acknowledges mistakes, and chooses mercy above judgment.

Through the lives of Miriam, Ruth, Grace, Samuel, and Daniel, we witness how God’s love moves in tangible ways. It begins with a single act of compassion and extends across time, touching generations and transforming hearts.

This book invites reflection on your own life. It calls you to consider how love has healed you, how mercy has found you, and how your life can become a channel of that same love.

May these pages remind you that healing is possible, forgiveness is powerful, and love, when lived with intention, never fails.
