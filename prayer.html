Prayer of Commitment

Lord Jesus, I come before You today with a heart that longs to be healed and a life that longs to reflect Your love. I commit myself to letting Your love cover every wound, every mistake, and every regret in my life.

Teach me to forgive as You forgive. Help me to show mercy where I might otherwise judge. Give me the courage to love even when it is difficult, to reach out even when I feel unworthy, and to trust that Your grace is sufficient for every broken place in me.

I surrender my past, my pain, and my fears into Your hands. Heal what has been hidden, restore what has been lost, and use my life as a vessel of Your love and compassion. Let my actions, my words, and my heart be a testimony of Your mercy, covering others in love just as You have covered me.

Lord, I commit to walking in Your love every day, trusting that it transforms, restores, and multiplies. May my life be a legacy of grace and hope, touching generations with the power of Your enduring love.

In Jesus’ name, amen.
