Prayer of Commitment

A moment to surrender, to heal, and to walk forward in love.

Lord Jesus, I come before You today with a heart that longs to be healed and a life that longs to reflect Your love. I commit myself to letting Your love cover every wound, every mistake, and every regret in my life.

Teach me to forgive as You forgive. Help me to show mercy where I might otherwise judge. Give me the courage to love even when it is difficult, to reach out even when I feel unworthy, and to trust that Your grace is sufficient for every broken place in me.

I surrender my past, my pain, and my fears into Your hands. Heal what has been hidden, restore what has been lost, and use my life as a vessel of Your love and compassion. Let my life reflect the mercy You have shown me.

I commit to walking in Your love daily, trusting that Your grace transforms, restores, and multiplies. Let my life become a testimony of hope and healing to others.

In Jesus’ name, amen.
